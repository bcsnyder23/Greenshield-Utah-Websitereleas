The Smarter Choice

Why Fiberglass?

A tougher, smoother pool that stays cleaner and handles freeze-thaw cycles better than concrete.

Faster Installation

Fiberglass shells are factory-manufactured and installed in days, not months. You're swimming sooner and spending less time in construction limbo.

Lower Maintenance

The smooth, non-porous surface resists algae growth, reduces chemical use, and eliminates the need for resurfacing every decade like plaster pools require.

Built for Utah

Fiberglass flexes with soil movement and freeze-thaw cycles, making it the ideal choice for Utah's variable climate. No cracking, no chipping.

Energy Efficient

Fiberglass retains heat better than concrete, reducing heating costs and keeping your pool at the perfect temperature for longer.

Comfortable & Safe

The smooth gelcoat finish is gentle on skin and feet, while built-in steps and benches provide a seamless, safe experience for every swimmer.

Long-Term Value

With lower lifetime maintenance costs and a durable shell that lasts 25+ years, fiberglass is the most cost-effective pool investment you can make.
