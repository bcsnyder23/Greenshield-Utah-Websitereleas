About Green Shield

Green Shield started in 1999 and over the years we have become one of Utah's premier specialty hardscape companies. Our goal is to make every client's hardscape dreams come true by building stunning hardscape features that make your property the oasis you deserve.

Our forte is residential hardscape installations that include specialty features such as rock walls, pavers, in-ground trampolines, fire pits, in-ground spas, along with the landscape lighting these features deserve. We look forward to having the opportunity to make your dream hardscape a reality.
